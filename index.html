About

I design products people use and businesses can measure.

My path into product design came through practice, not theory. I spent two years in customer success at a platform with 30,000 users, identifying friction, working with product and development teams, and watching how design decisions land in the real world. That grounding shapes how I work.

Since 2022 I've worked as a freelance UI/UX designer and no-code developer, designing and building end-to-end digital products for clients across SaaS, e-commerce, and local services.

I care about what happens after the design ships. How it lands with users and whether the metrics move.
